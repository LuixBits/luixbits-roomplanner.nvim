# Code of conduct

RoomPlan contributors and maintainers are expected to keep participation safe,
professional, and focused on constructive collaboration.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, patient, and specific when discussing code or design.
- Critique ideas and observable behavior rather than people.
- Welcome different experience levels and ask for clarification in good faith.
- Respect privacy and remove personal plan data from public reports.
- Accept maintainers' decisions about project scope while remaining free to
  document a technical disagreement respectfully.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, deliberate
intimidation, repeated personal attacks, publishing private information, and
disrupting project spaces are not tolerated. Spam, knowingly misleading
reports, and using project channels to pressure individuals are also outside
the project standard.

## Scope and enforcement

This policy applies in repository issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions,
release communication, and other spaces where someone represents RoomPlan.
Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, limit participation, temporarily
ban, or permanently ban behavior that violates this policy. Enforcement should
be proportional, documented privately, and avoid exposing reporters or other
sensitive information.

Report conduct concerns privately through the contact route in
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md), beginning the report title with `Conduct:`. Do not
open a public issue containing sensitive details. Good-faith reports will not
result in retaliation.
